Complete a standardised process designed to help simplify buying for your school or schools.

You will create a series of documents that can be used to help get authorisiation to buy for your school in a legal and complaint way.

Work through a series of steps to:

  • plan how to buy for your school - get help understanding what you need to do, who to involve and the things you need to think about.
  • create a specification for what your school needs to buy - gather your shool's requirements and start to draft a specification that you'll be able to share with staff and suppliers
  • select a route to market when buying for your school - identify approprate ways to fufill your buying needs
  • get help speaking to suppliers about your school's buying needs - speak to suppliers in a compliant way to share your needs and refine your inviation to tender
  • get help advertising your school's contract for tender - find out recommended ways to advertise your tender

You will be able to start an new or resume an existing procurement.
