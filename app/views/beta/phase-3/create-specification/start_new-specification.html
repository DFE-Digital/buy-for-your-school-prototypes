Create a specification to procure a catering service for your school

This tool will help you to create a specification for a single school.

A specification is a document which provides details about what your school wants a supplier to provide as part of a catering service.

You can share the specification with suppliers to help them understand what the school wants to achieve.

Suppliers can use this information to decide whether they are able to provide the service and if they are interested in working with your school.

This service will guide you to provide your requirements for a new service. It will also automatically include standard requirements that all school catering suppliers must meet.

You will be able to download an editable specification at the end of this tool.
