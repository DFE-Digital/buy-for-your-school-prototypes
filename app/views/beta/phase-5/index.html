A guide to buying and procuring for schools.

If you need additional support with a procurement

Get free help from a Department for Education procurement advisor or a network of people responsible for procurement in schools.

First steps of any procurement

  • 1. Check you're ready to start a procurement

    Steps you may need to take before starting a procurement, including writing business cases, understanding costs and assessing the market.

  • 2. Understand your school's needs for a procurement

    Find out who to speak to and what information to collect to understand your school's objectives and prepare for creating a specification to share with suppliers.

  • 3. Find the right procurement process

    Check which procurement process you need to follow based on how much you're spending, and find out how much time you may need for each process.

  • 4. Create a specification for your school

    Get advice and access our Create a specification service to build a comprehensive document that explains to suppliers what your school needs.

Next steps depending on your procurement process

Only follow one of these step by step processes.

There are rules about which processes you can follow depending on how much you're spending. Other factors like how much time you have may influence your decision.

Make sure you've completed the Find the right procurement process step before you continue.

  • Choose a supplier from a framework, step by step

    How to use Find a framework to select a quality-checked supplier. Frameworks use pre-written terms and conditions that are approved by DfE.

  • Run a mini competition from a framework, step by step

    How to use Find a framework to get 3 quotes from quality-checked suppliers. Frameworks use pre-written terms and conditions that are approved by DfE.

  • Get at least 3 quotes from suppliers, step by step

    How to procure low to medium value goods and services by choosing at least 3 suppliers to get a quote from.

  • Advertise a contract and run a procurement process, step by step

    How to procure high value goods and services, including how to advertise a contract and go to tender.

  • Run a compliant procurement process, step by step

    How to procure high value goods and services in compliance with EU procurement rules, including how to advertise a contract and go to tender.
