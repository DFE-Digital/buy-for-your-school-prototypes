How to buy goods or services for your school.

Following these guides will help schools to:

  • get value for money
  • comply with procurement law

Read buying for schools for advice on the types of goods and services schools regularly buy, such as catering and ICT hardware.

On 31 December 2020, the Find a Tender service replaced Tenders Electronic Daily (TED) in the UK public procurement process. UK buyers who previously published OJEU notices on TED should now use the Find a Tender service. Notices published before this change are still on TED.

  • Find the right way to buy

  • Writing a specification

    How schools can buy from a framework agreement where all suppliers can bid.

  • Buying high-value things under the EU procurement threshold

  • Buying things that are over the procurement threshold
